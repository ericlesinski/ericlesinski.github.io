THE BAND

Fully formed in Philadelphian suburbia in 2014, Brick Nova consists of Paul Dallas, Eric Lesinski, Jared Filer, and Tom Drakeford. Crafting an amalgam of garage-esque power pop, rock, punk, and alternative, Brick Nova has managed to create dynamic timbres ranging from heavy, hard-hitting grunge to eloquent and soft-spoken acoustic. Their debut LP, On The Move, was released in August of 2016. Since then, the group has been gigging, practicing, and recording despite attending college and working far away from each other. The group’s second album, Play Dead, was released in June 2018. An hour-long, 17-track journey to your rock'n'roll happy place, Play Dead shows frontman Tom Drakeford's development as a producer and audio engineer. Brick Nova records their studio albums independently in their rehearsal space/studio.
